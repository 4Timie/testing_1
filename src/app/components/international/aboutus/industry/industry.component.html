eProcess International S.A is a specialised subsidiary of Ecobank Transnational Incorporated (ETI). The company was incorporated in 2002 and has its head office 1310 North Courthouse Road, Suite 890. Arlington, VA 22201.

To meet the business value propositions for digital banking and cost-effective software gave rise to the need for a dedicated software team within the enterprise

eProcess was established with the focus of developing Ecobank proprietary software, business process innovations and service enhancement. The goals will lead to improving time to market, reducing software expenditure cost annually and becoming the innovation hub of the bank whilst gradually maturing into a full fintech organization.

Presently, eProcess provides technology services to ECOBANK affiliates from two sites (Accra and Lagos).
